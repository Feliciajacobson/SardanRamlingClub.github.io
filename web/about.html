About US

Our Vision

Ours is a vision in which hiking, foot trails, and their surrounding natural areas inspire Americans to get outdoors, volunteer and protect trails. As the national voice for hikers, Sardan Rambling Club will promote hiking, build a nationwide constituency of hikers and trail stewards, form strategic alliances with national, regional and local partners and advocate for hikers in the nation’s capital. Sustainable hiking trails and trail systems will bring people together, connect them with our natural and cultural heritage, promote healthy lifestyles and serve an integral role in conservation. We will expand our nation’s hiking community and motivate, recruit and educate tomorrow’s hiking leaders, advocates, and volunteers.

Our Mision

Empowering all to enjoy, share, and preserve the hiking experience.

What is Rambling?

Rambling is the activity of walking in the countryside for pleasure.

What is Sardan Rambling about?

The Ramblers is a charity dedicated to removing barriers so everyone can enjoy walking in green spaces and to preserving and improving hundreds of thousands of miles of well-loved paths, tracks and trails across England, Scotland, and Wales.

What do we offer?

A rambling club offers organized group hikes, social interaction, physical and mental health benefits, opportunities to learn navigation and outdoor skills, environmental education, special events like themed hikes and charity walks, and access to resources on walking routes, safety, and equipment. It helps members enjoy the outdoors, stay active, and make new friends.

What are the Health Benefits?

Joining a rambling club improves cardiovascular health, aids in weight management, and strengthens muscles and bones. It enhances joint flexibility, reduces stress and anxiety, fosters social connections, improves sleep quality, boosts the immune system, and increases overall energy levels.
